A Business Unit is a unit of an enterprise that performs one or many business functions and a unit of an enterprise that can be consolidated in both a managerial and legal hierarchy.  Other than that, it is a loosely defined unit of an organisation and it is left to the users of the application to decide how business units should be constructed and used.
