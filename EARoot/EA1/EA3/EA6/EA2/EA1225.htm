A key piece of interaction information is the participants that represent both sides of the communication.  An interaction may have zero or more participants associated with it.  These participants represent the individuals involved in that particular communication.  There may be one or more internal representatives of the organisation (e.g. Resources, Agents, Sales representatives, etc.), or one or more external representatives (e.g. Contacts of a Customer).  Interactions support only one customer per Interaction but can have multiple contacts for the customer.
