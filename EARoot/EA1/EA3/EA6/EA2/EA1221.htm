Business object association capability allows interactions to have linkage to other business objects that are involved in an interaction. Multiple object associations are supported. For each association, the action taken against the business object can be captured. At the time of interaction creation or update, users can select a value from the lookup table to specify whether the business object is created/updated/viewed/re-opened/closed.
