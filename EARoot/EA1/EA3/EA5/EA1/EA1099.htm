Notes: Indicates the PerQuantity side of this Amount. Typically, this value is 1 with a uom of each, meaning that, e.g., a UnitPrice with the Amount 1.29 would be the price per each or simply $1.29 each. For pricing wire, the UnitPrice Amount might be $7.00 with a  PerQuantity of 100 and a uom of LinearFeet, meaning the unit price would be $7 per 100 linear feet.
