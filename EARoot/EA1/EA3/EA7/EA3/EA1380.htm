Received Payment - A Received Payment EBO encapsulates a Payment transaction between one payer account and one payee account. The deploying organization acts as creditor for the purpose of this EBO and hence this Payment is incoming to the Accounts Receivable system of the deploying company. One Received Payment transaction may pay more than one Accounts Receivable Document in creditor's system.
