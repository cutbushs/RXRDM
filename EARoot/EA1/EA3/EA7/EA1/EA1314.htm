Identifies the payment term for an Order or Invoice. Supports the ability to specify an absolute due date, a number of days from a start day, a percent discount or an absolute discount. <br /></p><p>Amount is the fixed discount amount applied if paid by the DueDateTime for example, $20 off if paid by due date.<br /></p><p>Percent is the discount applied if paid by DueDateTime<br /></p>
