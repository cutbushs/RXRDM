<font color="#663300"><b>Resource the Organization and Develop Staff (N01)</b></font><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Meet company manpower requirements by recruitment, transfer, development, training and release of staff. Develop staff towards their potential. </li><br /></p><p><li>Develop a company that is constantly improving its learning capability. </li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p><p><b>N0101 Analyse Future Human Resources Requirements:</b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/futurerequirem] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Define the company human resources requirements against different staff structure scenarios, in terms of educational discipline, experience, competence, numbers of staff (expatriate / local), types of staff (company or contractor) and required</li><br /></p><p></ul>timing.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p><b>N0202 Analyse Supply of Human Resources:</b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/supplyanalysis] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Identify and analyse the various human resources sources of supply, i.e. own company, affiliated companies and external labour market. </li><br /></p><p><li>Take into account employment demographics including staff structure, wastage and geographic location, competency profiles, succession plans and academic qualification trends. </li><br /></p><p><li>Identify current or potential human resources capability gaps which could erode the competitiveness of the company. </li><br /></p><p><li>Match human resources requirements to preferred sources of supply.</li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p><p><b>N0103 Analyse Attractiveness of Organization:</b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/organizationan] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Determine the employment preferences of key target audiences for recruitment in the labour market. </li><br /></p><p><li>Assess current level of the company's attractiveness, and that of competitors, and make internal and external plans for improvement.</li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p><p><b>N0104 Plan Manpower Resourcing:</b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/resourcingplan] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Assess company human resources requirements, overall and in detail, in both the short and long term. </li><br /></p><p><li>Review and specify which activities are core and should therefore be carried out by company staff, and which are non-core and can be carried out by third parties. </li><br /></p><p><li>Match company human resources requirements to preferred sources of supply, and growth in staff competencies, and thereby</li><br /></p><p></ul>define the company manpower resourcing strategy.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p><b>N0105 Plan Staff Development:</b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/staffdevelopme] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Plan staff for jobs and jobs for staff, to enhance development of potential of individual staff. </li><br /></p><p><li>Develop a human resources training and learning portfolio. </li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p><p><b>N0106 Maintain Staff Appraisal System: </b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/staffappraisal] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Develop and maintain ongoing process for setting company and individual performance targets and appraising performance against target expectations. </li><br /></p><p><li>Maintain consistent, equitable and documented procedure for mutual discussion on performance and competence. </li><br /></p><p><li>Rank staff potential and performance, collectively and comparatively.</li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p><p><b>N0107 Resource Manpower Requirements:</b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/staffresourcin] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Prepare a company staff recruitment plan for the attraction and recruitment of staff (company and contractor). </li><br /></p><p></ul><ul><li>Determine company manpower training requirements (companywide, functional and individual). Update the company job competency structure.</li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p><p><b>N0108 Develop Staff: </b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/staffdevelopme] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Develop staff towards their potential, via task assignment or training event, taking account of individual aspirations, expectations of growth in capacity, as well as company human resources requirements. </li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p><p><b>N0109 Maintain Training and Learning Programs: </b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/trainingprogra] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Create and update a human resources competency development program portfolio of competency development program (e.g. local and company-wide, functional and inter functional training courses, operator/technical training, distance-learning program, self-study modules under mentor supervision). This program/portfolio is applied to individuals on the basis of human resources training needs analysis (assessment of gap between required and actual competence profiles).</li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p><p><b>N0110 Recruit Staff: </b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/staffrecruitin] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Attract potential qualified candidates. </li><br /></p><p><li>Recruit staff according to company human resources requirements. </li><br /></p><p><li>Confirm openings and specifications; advertise postings; handle nominations and applications; conduct assessment activities</li><br /></p><p><li>(e.g. screening, tests, assessment centres, interviews etc.); reject or make offers; agree staff terms and sign agreements.</li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p><p><b>N0111 Induct Staff: </b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/staffinduction] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Induct staff into the company Prepare the company for arrival of employees; prepare employee information; place employees on payroll; carry out induction and training program; carry out welcome and orientation procedures.</li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p><p><b>N0112 Release Staff: </b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/staffreleasing] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Manage the termination of employment according to the relevant circumstances e.g. redundancy, dismissal, resignation, incapacity, death.</li><br /></p><p><li>Administer severance payments. </li><br /></p><p></ul>Apply end of service benefits/policies.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p><b>N0113 Transfer Staff: </b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/stafftransfera] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Carry out activities associated with the transfer of staff. </li><br /></p><p><li>Agree on staff moves between sending and receiving organizations; arrange hand-over; handle relocation; handle administration.</li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p><p><b>N0114 Provide Development Assignments etc.:</b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/developmentass] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Provide appropriate opportunities (based on planning of jobs or specific activities) to develop staff, e.g. expatriate or cross-functional development assignment, temporary attachment, special project or role. </li><br /></p><p><li>Consider long-term company human resources requirements as well as needs of individual staff; and ensure transparent and merit-based selection processes against limited opportunities.</li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p><p><b>N0115 Provide Training: </b><br /></p><p>[/humanresources/organizationalstaff/stafftraining] <br /></p><p><ul><li>Ensure that staff receives the most appropriate form of training/learning (event or process) in relation to their competence development need. </li><br /></p><p><li>Issue joining instructions and pre-work information; deliver training events; followup attendee action-plans; review course and propose improvements</li><br /></p><p></ul><br /></p>
