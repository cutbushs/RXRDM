There are points within a process where it is important to know whether a specific event as occurred or a condition has been met.  These events or conditions can be referred to as milestones.  The process model must be able to identify and react to the milestone.  There is more than one way to realize this pattern, depending on the exact nature of how the milestone will be used by the process.
