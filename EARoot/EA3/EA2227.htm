The Enterprise Data Warehouse (EDW) is a multi-dimensional database typically used for reporting and data analysis.  It is a central repository of data which is created by integrating data from one or more disparate sources. <br /></p><p>Data warehouses store current as well as historical data.  The business can use this database without concern for impacting day-to-day operations of the production database.<br /></p><p>The data stored in the warehouse are uploaded from the operational systems (such as marketing, sales etc.). <br /></p><p>The data may pass through an operational data store (ODS) for additional operations before they are used in the DWH for reporting.<br /></p><p>The EDW is normally used as part of a Business Intelligence (BI) capability, and is consequently owned and maintained by the BI team<br /></p>
