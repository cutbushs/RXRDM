At this time, vendors still tend to have multiple products in this space and the products tend to perform better in their primary area of functionality.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>In this context, ETL (Extract, Transform Load) can also refer to EL-T (Or Extract, Load - Transform).<br /></p>
