Scenario Type
Detailed Path Basic Path
Details:
Steps:
1. Medical Practice sends Invoice to Administration Support
2. Administration Support records Invoice in Objective
3. Administration Support records Invoice in STRATIS
4. Administration Support creates payment in STRATIS
5. STRATIS pays Medical Practice (automatic payment run)
6. Administration Support requesst receipt from Medical Practice
7. Administration Support creates an action in STRATIS (PA-04)
8. Medical Practice sends receipt to Administration Support
9. Administration Support records communication in STRATIS
10. Administration Support creates claim
11. Administration Support sends claim form to Medicare
12. Administration Support sends claim form to Objective
13. Administration Support creates an action in STRATIS (PA-04)
14. Medicare sends payment to Administration Support
15. Administration Support record payment in STRATIS
Basic Path Basic Path
Details:
Steps:
1. Medical Practice sends Invoice to STL
2. STL pays Invoice amount to Medical Practice
3. STL requests receipt from Medical Practice
4. Medical Practice send receipt to STL
5. STL create claim form
6. STL send claim gap form to Medicare
7. Medicare send Cheque to STL
8. STL bank payment
